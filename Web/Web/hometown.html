Top Tourist Attractions in Ernakulam

Mattancherry Palace

Built in 1557 by the Portuguese, this palace was a gift to Raja Veera Kerala Varma of Kochi. It features Kerala murals depicting the Rajas of Kochi.

Learn more

Hill Palace of Tripunithura

The largest archaeological museum in the state, showcasing Kerala's rich heritage. Built in 1865, it consists of 49 buildings spread across 54 acres.

Learn more

Fort Kochi

A popular tourist destination known for its historic charm and architecture, with attractions like the Chinese fishing nets and colonial buildings.

Learn more

Bolgatty Palace

The oldest palace built by the Dutch outside of Holland, now a resort featuring beautiful gardens and scenic views.

Learn more

Cherai Beach

A beautiful beach known for its calm waters, ideal for swimming and dolphin sightings. A perfect spot for relaxation.

Learn more

Bhoothathan Kettu Dam & Reserve Forest

A picturesque dam and reserve forest in Ernakulam district, perfect for nature lovers and adventure seekers.
